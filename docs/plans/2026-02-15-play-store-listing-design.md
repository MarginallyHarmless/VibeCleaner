# Play Store Listing — Descriptions

## Title (39 characters)

Stash or Trash: Photo & Gallery Cleaner

## Short Description (80 characters)

Swipe to clean your gallery. Find duplicates, blurry & dark photos. Free up space!

## Full Description

**Swipe your photo gallery clean.** Stash or Trash is a fast, fun photo cleaner that lets you organize your gallery with simple swipes — right to keep, left to delete. Undo anytime if you change your mind.

Tired of scrolling through thousands of photos you'll never look at again? This gallery cleaner makes it easy to quickly delete unwanted photos and free up storage space on your phone.

**Swipe to clean your gallery:**
- Swipe right to keep a photo
- Swipe left to delete it
- Undo anytime — nothing is permanent until you confirm

**Browse your way:**
- By date — start from your oldest or newest photos
- By album — clean up one folder at a time
- All media — see everything at once
- Random shuffle — for when you want to mix it up

**Smart Scanner — find problem photos automatically:**
The built-in scanner detects duplicate photos, similar photos, blurry photos, too-dark shots, and screenshots. Review what it finds and bulk delete in seconds. No more wasting storage on photos you don't need.

**Organize as you go:**
Move photos between albums while swiping — a photo organizer and gallery cleaner in one app. No need to switch to a file manager.

**Track your cleanup progress:**
Built-in stats show how many photos you've reviewed, kept, and deleted, plus how much storage space you've freed up.

**Your privacy matters:**
Everything happens on your device. No cloud uploads, no data collection, no accounts. Your photos stay private.

**Why Stash or Trash?**
- Fast & fun — clean your gallery in minutes, not hours
- Smart duplicate photo finder built in
- Detects blurry, dark, and low-quality photos automatically
- Screenshot cleaner to remove old captures
- Works offline — no internet required
- Lightweight — won't slow down your phone
- Free up space without losing the photos you love

Download Stash or Trash and start decluttering your photo gallery today.

## Design Decisions

- **Tone**: Fun & casual
- **Lead pain point**: Deleting photos is tedious
- **Feature focus**: Swipe experience first, scanner as supporting feature
- **Premium**: Not mentioned in listing — all features listed together
